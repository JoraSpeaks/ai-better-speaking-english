# Flashcard Images Directory

This directory contains images to accompany vocabulary flashcards.
Each image should be 400x300 pixels, PNG format, and illustrate the vocabulary concept.

## Required Images:

### Greetings Category:
- good-morning.png - Sunrise or person waving
- how-are-you.png - Two people talking
- nice-to-meet-you.png - Handshake or introduction scene

### Travel Category:
- boarding-pass.png - Airplane ticket/boarding pass
- departure-gate.png - Airport gate area
- taxi.png - Yellow taxi cab

### Dining Category:
- menu.png - Restaurant menu
- bill-check.png - Receipt or bill
- appetizer.png - Small dish or starter food

### Shopping Category:
- receipt.png - Shopping receipt
- sale-discount.png - Sale tag or discount sign
- credit-card.png - Credit/debit card

### Hotel Category:
- reception.png - Hotel front desk
- housekeeping.png - Cleaning cart or maid service
- wifi-password.png - WiFi symbol or router

### Emergency Category:
- emergency.png - Emergency sign or first aid
- pharmacy.png - Pharmacy store or medical cross
- prescription.png - Medicine bottle or prescription pad

### Common Phrases:
- excuse-me.png - Person getting attention politely
- help.png - Person asking for assistance

## Image Guidelines:
- Size: 400x300 pixels
- Format: PNG with transparent background
- Style: Clean, simple illustrations
- Colors: Use theme colors (#4A90E2, #50E3C2)
- Content: Clear visual representation of the concept
